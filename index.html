My name is Emily Tsai. I am currently an undergraduate at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science.
With my degree in computer science, I hope to bridge technology with community work, and use coding to promote welfare in underserved/under-resourced communities. As an ex-biology major and current CS tutor, I have a particularly strong interest in working in the health and education fields.
