Track 158: Taxis

Python, Max

A data "audio"-lization of New York City taxis.

This program follows the unique path of a taxi for 24 hours as it travels across New York. It translates the taxi's changing location into sound based on its latitude and longitude coordinates. The resulting sequence of melodies outlines the unique bundle of trips that a taxi driver makes each day.

Cal Day Scheduler

Ruby on Rails

Easily personalize and save events for Cal Day.

This web application was created with the purpose of making Cal Day--which brings in thousands of visitors for hundreds of events and booths--organizable and personizable for new students, current students, parents, and visitors. The Ink Initiative

The Ink Initiative website for The Daily Californian..
