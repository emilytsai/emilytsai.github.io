Hi! My name is Emily Tsai.

I'm currently an undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science.

With my degree in computer science, I hope to bridge technology with social welfare and community work in underserved/under-resourced communities. I have a particularly strong interest in doing collaborative work in the fields of health and education.
