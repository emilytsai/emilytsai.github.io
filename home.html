Hello, World!

I'm Emily Tsai.

I'm a UC Berkeley computer science major with a passion for creative work, community work, education, and puzzles. Every day I'm excited by the coding that I get to do; but even more so than the technology itself, I care about the problems that technology can solve.

Outside of my interests in the world of bits and algorithms, I also enjoy leading multiple lives in imaginary places (reading novels), training my mind and body to endure hours of pain (running half-marathons), and learning/sharing highly interesting--but often irrelevant--coffee table facts. (Did you know that human beings have more bacterial cells in our bodies than we have of our own human cells? You are more not-you, than you are you!) Most of all, I love keeping company with my family and friends: hiking, playing board games, and eating! :)
