the ink initiative

Team: Ben Gee (Online Developer), Danielle Kenwood (Layout Design), Emily Tsai (Online Developer)
Website: [ink.dailycal.org]

The Purpose

The Daily Cal newspaper operates independently of UC Berkeley; this is, in part, due to the 2012-initiated UC Berkeley student fee that would apply for the last time in 2017. As its sunset approached, The Daily Cal acknowledged that it would not be able to continue to produce the same level of journalism without support from the student body for the next few years.

Thus, the Ink Initiative was created. The campaign was the 2016 ASUC ballot referendum that would institute a semesterly student fee to help the newspaper support its operations to provide daily news coverage in the Berkeley community. To do so, the referendum needed a website to provide a portal for information on the fee and all its details, as well as provide links for the students to contact a knowledgeable Daily Cal staff for more information and vote on the ballot.

The Vision, The Reality

To create the web page, Danielle first drafted up the page layouts, provided fonts, and included notes on interactive portions of the page. Ben and I then turned to HTML, CSS, and Javascript to make the vision a reality. Built from a blank slate, the page was created in close replica of the original layout design and then continually updated to support the initiative's many transitions through its campaign timeline. The webpage is now archived, but it sought its beginning stages whilst calling students to sign the petition getting the referendum on the ASUC ballot, and paved its way through the final stages of the ASUC voting period.
