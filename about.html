OUT AND ABOUT

Hi! My name is Emily Tsai.

I'm currently an undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying Computer Science.

With my degree in computer science, I hope to bridge technology with social welfare and community work. A place that celebrates creativity, passion, and a do-good attitude is somewhere I'm happy to be.

Outside of my interests in the world of bits and algorithms, I also enjoy leading multiple lives in imaginary places (reading novels), training my mind and body to endure hours of pain (running half-marathons), and learning/sharing highly interesting--but often irrelvant--coffee table facts. (Did you know that human beings have more bacterial cells in our bodies than we have of our own human cells? You are more not-you, than you are you!) Most of all, I love keeping company with my family and friends: hiking, playing board games, and eating! :)

Thanks for visiting my page! Please feel free to explore--I've tucked away some of my favorite tidbits of facts here and there.
